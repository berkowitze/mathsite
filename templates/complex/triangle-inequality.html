The Triangle Inequality
$1. \left | z_0+z_1\right | \le \left | z_0\right | + \left | z_1 \right |$ Proof
$2. \left | z_0+z_1\right | \ge \left | z_0\right | - \left | z_1 \right |$ Proof
$3. \left | z_0-z_1\right | \ge \left | z_0\right | - \left | z_1 \right |$ Proof
$4. \left | z_1 + z_2 + z_3 \right | \le |z_1| + |z_2| + |z_3|$ Proof

Proof of Triangle Inequality: $| z_0+z_1 | \le | z_0 | + | z_1 |$.

Before beginning the proof, recall:

  1. $z\,\bar{z} = |z|^2$ and $|z| = |-z|$
  2. $\left |z_1z_2\right | = \left | z_1\right |\left |z_2\right|$
  3. $\Re \{z\} = \frac {z + \bar{z}} {2}$
  4. $|\Re \{z\}| \le |z|$

\begin{align} \left | z_0 + z_1 \right |^2 & = \left (z_0 + z_1\right )\left (\overline{z_0 + z_1}\right ) \\ & = \left (z_0 + z_1\right )\left (\overline{z_0} + \overline{z_1}\right ) \\ & = z_0\overline{z_0} + z_1\overline{z_1} + \overline{z_0}z_1 + z_0\overline{z_1}. \end{align}
Notice that $\overline{z_0}z_1 = \overline{z_0\overline{z_1}}.$ Therefore,
\begin{align} | z_0 + z_1 |^2 & = z_0\overline{z_0} + z_1\overline{z_1} + \overline{z_0\overline{z_1}} + z_0\overline{z_1} \\ & = |z_0|^2 + |z_1|^2 + 2\Re \{z_0z_1\} \\ & \le |z_0|^2 + z_1^2 + 2|z_0z_1| \\ & = (|z_0|+|z_1|)^2 \end{align}
So we have
$|z_0+z_1|^2 \le (|z_0|+|z_1|)^2.$
Take the sqaure root of both sides to get the triangle inequality:
$|z_0+z_1|\le |z_0| + |z_1|$
